what is the purpose of viewport meta tag in
The viewport meta tag in HTML tells the browser how to adjust a web page's dimensions and scaling to suit the device it's being viewed on. This helps ensure that the page is displayed correctly and responsively on a variety of devices and screen resolutions, especially mobile devices.
